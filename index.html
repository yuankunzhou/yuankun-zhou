Yuankun Zhou 周元坤

Artist · Dramaturg · Playwright · Researcher

Yuankun Zhou is an artist, dramaturg, playwright, and researcher based in Rotterdam. Their practice moves between language, stage, and material form.

They are interested in collaborative authorship, archival instinct, and the politics of who gets to tell a story.
